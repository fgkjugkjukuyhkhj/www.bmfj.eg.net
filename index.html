Journal of Art, Design and Music

Helwan University (HU) is a public university established in 1975, and located in Cairo, Egypt. Although HU is just 46 years old university, its faculties such as “Applied Arts”, “Fine Arts”, “Art Education” and “ Music” were established in 1839, 1908, and 1936 respectively, far before the official HU establishment. Therefore, HU is considered to be a unique model among Egyptian universities as it encompasses the oldest and the most prestigious Art, Design, and Music faculties in the MENA region.

The Journal of Art, Design and Music (JADM) aims to improve quality of Art, Design and Music scientific research, applying international standards. The journal also gives researchers from all over the world the opportunity to publish inter-national research within arts, Design & music scope. The JADM themes include many scientific fields covering all sub disciplines of Art, Design & Music. It is distinguished by research seeking to solve problems that need analytical, innovative and creative studies, as well as philosophical approaches.
